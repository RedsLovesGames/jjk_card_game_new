## Key Observations
1. **Stat System**: ATK/DEF/HP with DEF likely serving as both defense and health
2. **Energy System**: Cost and Ultimate_Cost for resource management
3. **Effect Text**: Plain English descriptions that need parsing
4. **Rarity System**: SSR, SR, R, C tiers
5. **Faction System**: Sorcerer, Cursed Spirit, etc.
6. **Ultimate Integration**: Ultimates tied to characters with separate costs
